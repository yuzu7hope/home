About me

PhD candidate at the Institute of Science, Tokyo, and a Research Associate (JRA) at the RIKEN Center for Bioscience and Biotechnology.
Her research focuses on implantation disorders and chromosomal abnormalities in oocytes, key contributors to infertility. She combines computational bioinformatics with molecular biology techniques to address biological gender disparities through biotechnology.
She is also actively involved in promoting social implementation and advancing diversity and inclusion to tackle societal challenges.
